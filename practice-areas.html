How I Can Help You

I handle a broad range of legal matters for clients in Guimaras and surrounding areas.

Criminal Law

I provide vigorous legal representation for individuals facing criminal charges under the Revised Penal Code and special penal laws of the Philippines. Whether you are under investigation, have been arrested, or are facing trial, I will work to protect your constitutional rights at every stage of the criminal process. Everyone deserves a competent defense, and I am committed to ensuring that your side is heard in court.

  • Crimes against persons (homicide, physical injuries)
  • Crimes against property (theft, robbery, estafa)
  • Cybercrime (RA 10175)
  • Violations of special penal laws
  • Bail applications
  • Appeals and post-conviction relief

Civil Law

I represent clients in a wide range of civil disputes under Philippine law, working to achieve fair and timely resolutions through negotiation, mediation, or litigation. Whether you are dealing with a property dispute, a breach of contract, or need to recover money owed to you, I will analyze your case thoroughly and advise you on the best legal strategy to protect your interests.

  • Property disputes
  • Contract disputes
  • Collection of sum of money
  • Damages and injury claims
  • Ejectment and unlawful detainer
  • Reconveyance and quieting of title

Labor Law

I assist employees and workers with employment-related legal matters, focusing on the protection of labor rights under the Philippine Labor Code.   • Unpaid wages and benefits

How I Can Help You

I handle a broad range of legal matters for clients in Guimaras and surrounding areas.

Criminal Law

I provide representation for individuals facing criminal charges. Everyone deserves a competent defense.

  • Crimes against persons (homicide, physical injuries)
  • Crimes against property (theft, robbery, estafa)
  • Cybercrime (RA 10175)
  • Violations of special penal laws
  • Bail applications
  • Appeals and post-conviction relief

Civil Law

I represent clients in a range of civil disputes, working to achieve fair resolutions through negotiation or litigation.

  • Property disputes
  • Contract disputes
  • Collection of sum of money
  • Damages and injury claims
  • Ejectment and unlawful detainer
  • Reconveyance and quieting of title

Annulment

I handle annulment cases with compassion and discretion, guiding clients through the legal process of declaring a marriage void.

  • Declaration of nullity of marriage
  • Annulment of voidable marriages
  • Psychological incapacity cases
  • Legal separation
  • Custody and support arrangements
  • Property settlement

Real Estate

I assist clients with real estate transactions, land disputes, and property-related legal matters under Philippine law.

  • Land title verification and transfer
  • Deed of sale and contract to sell
  • Extrajudicial settlement of estate
  • Land disputes and boundary issues
  • Lease agreements
  • Tax declarations and property documentation

Labor Law

I assist with employment-related legal matters, protecting the rights of workers and advising on workplace compliance.

  • Illegal dismissal cases
  • Unpaid wages and benefits
  • Labor standards violations
  • NLRC and DOLE complaints
  • Employment contracts
  • Workplace harassment and disputes

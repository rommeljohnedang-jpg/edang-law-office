Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last updated: February 7, 2026

Information We Collect

When you use our website or contact form, we may collect the following information: your name, email address, phone number, and the details of your legal inquiry. This information is provided voluntarily when you submit a consultation request or contact us directly.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely for the purpose of responding to your legal inquiry, scheduling consultations, and providing legal services. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Attorney-Client Privilege

Information shared during a consultation or in the course of legal representation is protected by attorney-client privilege under Philippine law. We treat all client communications as strictly confidential.

Contact Form Submissions

Our contact form is processed through FormSubmit.co, a third-party form handling service. The information submitted through our contact form is sent directly to our office email. We do not store your form submissions on our website's server.

Website Analytics

We may use analytics tools to understand how visitors use our website. This data is collected anonymously and helps us improve the website experience. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites such as Google Maps and Google Reviews. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party websites. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Your Rights

Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173), you have the right to access, correct, and request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at info@edanglaw.com.
